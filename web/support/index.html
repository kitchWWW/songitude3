Songitude — Support

Songitude is a location-based sound walk player. Put on headphones, open a walk, press play, and the audio changes as you move through the mapped area.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and allow location when prompted (choose "While Using the App").
  2. Pick a walk from the list (sorted by what's nearest you).
  3. Press the big play button and start walking. Audio keeps playing in the background with your screen locked and the phone in your pocket.

Common questions

Why does it need my location? Only to play the right sounds for where you're standing and to sort nearby walks. Your location never leaves your device — see our Privacy Policy. The audio stopped when I locked my phone. Make sure you granted location access and pressed play. The app is designed to keep rendering in the background; it only stops when you pause or fully quit the app. Can I listen without the app? Yes — there's a web player, but it can't run in the background, so keep the page open and screen on. The iOS app is the full experience. I want to publish my own sound walks.
