Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Songitude is a location-based audio experience: it plays different sounds depending on where you are physically standing. This policy explains exactly what data is involved.

Your location, in plain terms: we request access to your location only to deliver the audio experience (playing the right sounds for where you're standing) and to sort the "sound walks near you" list. Your location is used in real time on your device — we do not collect it, transmit it to our servers, store it, log it, sell it, or share it.

Location data

When you play a sound walk, the app (or web player) reads your device's location continuously to decide which sounds you should hear. This processing happens on your device. The "walks near you" ordering is computed on your device using your most recent location. At no point is your location sent to Songitude's servers or any third party.

What we do store

The only personal data we store is for creators who publish walks:

  • Google account email — used solely to check that you're approved to publish, and recorded as the owner of walks you publish so you can manage them.
  • Walk content you upload — the audio, map shapes, title, creator name, and description you choose to publish. These are made publicly available in the app's catalog by design.

If you only listen, you don't sign in and we store nothing about you.

No tracking or ads

Songitude contains no advertising, no analytics/tracking SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

Third-party services

  • Amazon Web Services (S3, Lambda) — hosts published walks and runs a small serverless function that verifies a creator's Google sign-in when publishing. It never receives your location.
  • Google Sign-In — used only to authenticate approved creators. Governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Map tiles (OpenStreetMap / CARTO) — used to draw the map. Standard tile requests do not include your precise location.

Children

Songitude is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date.
